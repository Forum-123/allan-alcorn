Allan at ATARI

Allan's role at Atari cannot be overstated, he is responsible for designing one of the earliest video games to be produced... the now legendary PONG. But further than PONG, Allan was also involved in numerous other projects while working at Atari:

  • Responsible for hiring Steve Jobs at Atari when Jobs was only 18
  • Worked on the games system Cosmos that never reached release, it was designed to be a handheld console, unlike other games systems of the time which needed to be plugged into a screen

ATARI's Legacy

Atari, inc. was an American video game developer and home computer company founded in 1972 by Nolan Bushnell and Ted Dabney. Atari was responsible for the formation of the video arcade and modern video game industry.
